Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Data We Collect

Spendly collects personal and financial information that you voluntarily provide when using our service. This includes your name, email address, account credentials, and expense records that you choose to log. We do not collect sensitive financial information such as credit card numbers or bank account details directly.

How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely for providing and improving our expense tracking functionality. Your information enables us to display your expense history, generate spending summaries, and provide category-based insights. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers with industry-standard encryption and access controls. We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. Data is retained for as long as your account remains active.

Third Party Services

Spendly may use third-party service providers for hosting, analytics, and customer support. These partners have access to your data only to perform specific tasks on our behalf and are obligated to maintain confidentiality. We do not share your expense data with advertisers or data brokers.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at privacy@spendly.example.com. You may also request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time through your account settings.
